The page displays a visualization of the Mandelbrot set. For information about the Mandelbrot set, check out Wikipedia's article on the subject.

Every frame, for each pixel, the program calculates one more item in the Mandelbrot sequence that corresponds to the complex number that corresponds to that pixel. If a pixel "escapes" (if the sequence exceeds 2), it is drawn in red. Lighter red indicates that it took more iterations to escape.

Each number at the bottom is the number of iterations that have been calcualated for a particular chunk of the pixels. The visualization is broken into seven chunks that are all analyzed in parallel.

Press "r" to reset the visualization.
